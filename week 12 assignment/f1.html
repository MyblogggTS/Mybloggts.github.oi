Formula 1

F1 also known as Formula One is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars,The word formula in the name refers to the set of rules all participant cars must follow. A Formula One season consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix.

Grands Prix take place in multiple countries and continents on either purpose-built circuits or closed roads. The four most difficult circuits are: Circuit De Monaco, Circuit De Spa-Francorchamps, Marina Bay Street Circuit, and Hungaroring. These circuits are the most difficult because the shape of the track and how much G-force the drivers go through and the way the sharp turns. The FIA Formula One World Championship has been one of the world's premier forms of motorsport since its inaugural running in 1950 and is often considered to be the pinnacle of motorsport. Oscar Piastri

The Australian racing driver Oscar Jack Piastri who competes in Formula One for team McLaren as the second driver. He has won a total of five Formula One Grand Prix across three seasons. He started his racing career at age nine in Karting.

Max Verstappen

learn more about Max Verstappen.

The Dutch racing driver Max Emilian Verstappen also known as,"Mad Max" who competes in Formula One for team RedBull as the First driver. He has won a total of Sixty-four Formula One Grand Prix and has won four World Drivers' Championship titles, which he won consecutively from 2021 to 2024.

Charles Leclerc

learn more about Charles Leclerc.

The Monegasque racing driver Charles Marc Leclerc who competes in Formula One for team Scuderia Ferrari. He has won a total of eight Formula One Grand Prix and was runner-up in the Formula World Drivers' Championship in 2022 with Ferrari.
